## Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

### Do’s
- Use professional language in public posts
- Verify information before sharing
- Maintain privacy settings
- Respect others’ opinions
- Share useful and positive content

### Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not spread misinformation
- Avoid engaging in online arguments
- Do not misuse someone else’s content without permission
