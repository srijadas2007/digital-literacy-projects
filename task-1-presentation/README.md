## Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic

In this task, I created a digital literacy infographic using Canva. The purpose of this infographic is to spread awareness about the importance of digital literacy among students.

The infographic covers key topics such as the meaning of digital literacy, useful digital tools, safe internet practices, maintaining a professional online presence, and proper email etiquette. I focused on keeping the design simple and visually clear by using short descriptions and relevant icons.

This task helped me understand how to present information in a concise and visually appealing way.
