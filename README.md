## Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic

In this task, I created a digital literacy infographic using Canva. The purpose of this infographic is to spread awareness about the importance of digital literacy among students.

The infographic covers key topics such as the meaning of digital literacy, useful digital tools, safe internet practices, maintaining a professional online presence, and proper email etiquette. I focused on keeping the design simple and visually clear by using short descriptions and relevant icons.

This task helped me understand how to present information in a concise and visually appealing way.

### Part A – Case Study

In this task, I researched UPI/Online Payment Fraud, a common type of cybercrime in India. The case study explains how attackers trick users into transferring money by creating urgency or impersonating trusted sources such as bank officials or service providers. It also describes the typical steps involved in such scams, the target audience, and the consequences of falling victim to these attacks.

### Part B – Prevention Checklist

I also created a “Stay Safe Online” checklist that includes practical and actionable steps to prevent cybercrime. The checklist focuses on safe digital practices, especially related to UPI transactions and online payments. It also includes official reporting channels such as the National Cyber Crime Portal and helpline number 1930.

This task helped me understand the importance of awareness and responsible behavior while using digital platforms.
